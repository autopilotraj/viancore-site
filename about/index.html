About VianCore
Built by someone who has
lived inside enterprise systems
for a long time.

VianCore exists because most enterprise knowledge lives in people's heads, buried in email threads, or locked inside organizations. This site is an attempt to fix that — one documented, refined, honest piece at a time.

The origin
18 years of questions nobody had good answers to.

Over nearly two decades working across enterprise systems and security, the same gaps kept appearing — not in technology, but in knowledge. Good documentation was rare. Honest accounts of what actually works were rarer. VianCore is the resource that didn't exist when it was needed most.

The approach
No theory. No vendor marketing. No fluff.

Everything published here comes from direct, hands-on experience. The frameworks, checklists, and guides are battle-tested — not assembled from other people's blog posts. If something didn't work in the real world, it doesn't appear here. That's the only filter that matters.
